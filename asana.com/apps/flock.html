What is Flock?

Stay on top of all your Asana activities by integrating Asana in Flock and start conversations around it! Always have context on tasks and never miss anything!

With the integration you can 1. Create tasks with just a quick /command 2. Monitor progress of all your projects 3. Get notified about tasks which have been completed, moved, comments added and much more!

Get Flock + Asana

Prerequisites - An active Asana and Flock account

Integrating Asana with your flock is super easy, just follow these steps

  1. Make sure you have active Asana and Flock accounts. Sign up for Flock here.
  2. Look for Asana in the Flock App store
  3. Install the app and connect your asana account to Flock
  4. Add your configuration to be notified in channels or in DMs
  5. Thats it!

Learn more and get support

To learn more about using the Flock Asana integration, visit the support page or email their team at support@flock.com
