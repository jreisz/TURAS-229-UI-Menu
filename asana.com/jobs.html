Growing fast to unlock an emerging market

All important progress requires teams of people to work together. But most progress is impeded by the difficulty of coordinating teams and lack of clarity about what needs to be done. Today, the world’s 1.25B knowledge workers (September 2019, Forrester) spend 60% of their time on work about work. Asana provides teams with a living system of clarity so they can focus on their most impactful work. With over 82,000 paying organizations, we’ve barely scratched the surface. We need curious, collaborative, and mission-driven people like you to help us enable the world’s teams to thrive.
